Our Professional Optometry Services

We are committed to providing you with the highest standard of eye care. Our services blend advanced technology with a personalized approach, ensuring your vision needs are met with precision and compassion.

Comprehensive Eye Exams

More than just a prescription check, our comprehensive eye exam is a deep dive into your ocular health. We utilize state-of-the-art diagnostic tools to ensure no detail is overlooked.

  • ✓Digital Retinal Imaging: We capture a high-resolution image of your retina, allowing us to detect early signs of conditions like diabetic retinopathy, glaucoma, and macular degeneration.
  • ✓Visual Field Testing: This test maps your peripheral vision to identify blind spots you might not notice, which can be an early indicator of glaucoma or other neurological issues.
  • ✓Tonometry: We measure your intraocular pressure (IOP) to assess your risk for glaucoma, a leading cause of irreversible blindness.

Advanced Contact Lens Fittings

Experience the freedom of frameless vision. Our contact lens service is tailored to your unique eyes and lifestyle, ensuring optimal comfort, clarity, and health.

  • ✓Specialty Lens Expertise: We fit all types of lenses, including toric lenses for astigmatism, multifocal lenses for presbyopia, and scleral lenses for complex conditions like keratoconus.
  • ✓Corneal Topography: We map the unique curvature of your cornea to ensure a perfect, custom fit, which is crucial for comfort and sharp vision.
  • ✓Patient Education: We provide comprehensive training on insertion, removal, and hygiene to ensure you can wear your lenses safely and confidently.

Designer Eyewear & Styling

Your eyewear is a reflection of your personality. Our optical boutique is a curated space where fashion meets function, featuring frames from leading designers and independent labels.

  • ✓Personalized Styling: Our experienced team will help you select frames that complement your face shape, skin tone, and personal style, ensuring you leave feeling confident and seeing perfectly.
  • ✓Premium Lens Technology: We offer the latest in lens technology, including thinner and lighter high-index lenses, advanced anti-reflective coatings, blue light protection for digital device users, and adaptive Transitions® lenses.
  • ✓Custom Fittings & Adjustments: We provide lifetime adjustments and cleaning to ensure your eyewear always fits perfectly and feels comfortable.

Ocular Disease Management

Our practice is equipped to diagnose and manage a wide range of ocular diseases. Early and accurate diagnosis is critical for protecting your vision from conditions that can cause irreversible damage.

  • ✓Glaucoma Management: Utilizing advanced imaging and visual field analysis, we monitor and treat glaucoma to control intraocular pressure and prevent optic nerve damage.
  • ✓Diabetic Eye Care: We provide essential annual screenings for diabetic patients to detect retinopathy early, helping to prevent vision loss associated with diabetes.
  • ✓Macular Degeneration: We use OCT scanning to monitor the health of your macula and provide guidance on nutritional supplements and lifestyle changes to manage AMD.
